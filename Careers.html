IIIT Naya Raipur is an institution of higher learning in Information and Communication Technology and related fields. It aspires to nurture and develop top notch scientists, engineers, leaders and entrepreneurs to meet the needs of the industry and society. We are actively looking for faculty and staff to join and contribute to this mission.

Certain positives of Naya Raipur region

  • This capital region is a hub of National and State level academic and research institutes including the Indian Institute of Management, AIIMS, HNLU, NIT, IG Agriculture University, Ravi Shankar University among several others.
  • Naya Raipur is the born smart city of modern India offering tier I city infrastructure away from the hustle and bustle of typical metro city.
  • The excellent infrastructure, cultural richness, low cost of living and good international schools in the region make it an ideal destination to support a family.
